Coffee

Coffee is a popular beverage enjoyed by people all over the world. It is made from roasted coffee beans and can be served hot or cold.
Coffee contains caffeine, which is a stimulant that can help improve mental alertness and reduce fatigue. There are many different types of coffee, including espresso, cappuccino, and latte, each with its own unique flavor and preparation method.
Coffee has a rich history and is an important part of many cultures,
often enjoyed during social gatherings or as a morning ritual.
